WHAT'S NEWS?

Match four keywords to get a news article.

How to Play

Find groups of four words that are part of a news headline.

  • Select four tiles and tap 'Submit' to check if your guess is correct.
  • Find the groups without making 4 mistakes!

About

What if we could burst our filter bubbles and enjoy news from a greater variety of publications?

‘What’s News?’ aims to break news consumption habits by turning the day’s headlines into a word game.

The game has no setter and is generated through automation. Each morning, 100 articles by the world’s most popular outlets are randomly selected from NewsAPI.

From those articles, four headlines are chosen at random and sent to OpenAI. For each headline, four keywords are generated and passed to the frontend for that day’s game.

Enjoy!
