How to Play

Find groups of four words that are part of a news headline.

  • Select four tiles and tap 'Submit' to check if your guess is correct.
  • Find the groups without making 4 mistakes!

About

‘What’s News?’ turns the day’s headlines into a word game. A new game is generated every day automatically.

Each morning, four articles from the world’s most popular outlets are randomly selected and sent to ChatGPT. It selects four words from each headline and sends them to the app for that day’s game.

Enjoy!
